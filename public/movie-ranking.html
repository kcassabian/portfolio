The Best Anime According to Boden

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Full Metal Alchemist

Two idiots perform forbidden alchemy in attempt to bring their dead mother back to life.

Death Note

Genius boy finds book capable of killing anybody, just by writing their name and picturing their face.

Code Geass

Mech, Psychological. Boy with the power of the king can command anybody to do anything.
